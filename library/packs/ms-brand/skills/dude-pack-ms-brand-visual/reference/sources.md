The four-square hex codes (`#F25022`, `#7FBA00`, `#00A4EF`, `#FFB900`) and the wordmark gray (`#737373`) are consistent across the public Microsoft Legal page, public asset packages, and third-party trackers.

## Precedence

If anything in this bundle ever conflicts with **Microsoft Brand Central**, **Brand Central wins**. This bundle is a convenience for internal Copilot workflows, not a source of truth.

## When to leave this bundle

Use the official guidelines instead of this bundle when:

- The artifact will be **published externally** (customer-facing site, partner co-marketing, ad, packaging).
- You're making a **logo lockup** with another brand.
- You're creating a **product icon**, badge, or trademark notice.
- You need **legal review**, font licensing, or partner-marketing guidance.

Contact: your team's brand contact, or `trademarks@microsoft.com` for trademark questions.
